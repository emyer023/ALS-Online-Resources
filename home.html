About ALS Online Resources

The project study is designed purposely to the Alternative Learning System in Sablayan National Comprehensive High School. The objective of the study is to develop an Alternative Learning System (ALS): Online Resources (HS) in which automated the record keeping where quizzes and assessments would be automatically saved in the user’s folder. It also provides resources just like softcopy of the module and video tutorial where they can watch the given video that is related to their lesson. Lastly, it provides a contact information where the users can talk to the ALS Teachers whenever they can’t understand what was written in the module.

The system designed to help ALS Teachers for them to easily teach or provide a softcopy of module to those students that is really want to learn even though they are far from the school or students with disabilities or what we called PWD. The system was developed using Visual Studio Code for the system development tool.
